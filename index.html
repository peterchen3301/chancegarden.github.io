What we’re about

We are a community volunteer organisation based in a century‑old Sanhe Yuan in Tamsui Danhai New Town. What began as a mission to protect this historic house has grown into a journey of exploring — and reimagining — Tamsui.

Our site is a living cultural space. We host art exhibitions, local‑history research, woodworking workshops, and guided tours for visitors of all ages and needs.

We Provide:
  • Historical & Cultural Tours
  • Walking & Eco Tours
  • Art Workshops & Exhibitions
  • Weekend Market

我們在做什麼

我們駐守淡海新市鎮百年古厝的社區組織，由守護古厝開始，到今天玩淡水遊古。這裡不只是歷史老房子，還有藝文展覽、文史研究、木作教學、淡水導覽，一起從公司田溪流域出發，開展屬於淡水在地的生活提案。

我們提供
  • 歷史與文化走讀
  • 生態導覽
  • 藝術工作坊與展覽
  • 週末市集
